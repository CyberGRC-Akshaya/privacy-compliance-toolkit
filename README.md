## 🎯 About

This toolkit provides **production-ready privacy compliance templates** developed from real DPO appointments across multiple regulated verticals including BFSI, IT consulting, software engineering, data science, and staffing.

**What makes this valuable:**
- **DPDPA India 2023 content** — almost nonexistent on GitHub; one of the first comprehensive repositories
- **GDPR ↔ DPDPA mapping** — shows regulatory alignment for multinational organisations
- **DPO operational templates** — DSAR management, breach notification workflows
- **Multi-jurisdiction comparison** — understand obligations across regions simultaneously

## 🗺️ Jurisdiction Coverage Map

| Law | Jurisdiction | Applies When | Key Right |
|-----|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| **GDPR** | EU/EEA + UK | Processing personal data of EU/UK residents | Right to erasure, portability |
| **DPDPA 2023** | India | Processing digital personal data in India | Consent, purpose limitation |
| **CCPA/CPRA** | California, USA | >$25M revenue, >50K records, or >50% revenue from data | Right to opt-out |
| **HIPAA** | USA | Healthcare providers, payers, and Business Associates | PHI protection |
| **PIPEDA** | Canada | Federal private sector organisations | Access, correction |
| **ISO 27701** | International | Extension to ISO 27001 for privacy | PIMS certification |
